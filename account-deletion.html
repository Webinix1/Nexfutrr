üì± Step-by-Step Deletion Process

Follow these simple steps to delete your account:

Step 1: Open the Profile Section

Launch the app and navigate to your Profile section. You can usually find this by tapping on your profile icon or accessing the main menu.

Step 2: Locate Delete Account Button

Scroll down in your profile settings until you find the Delete Account button. This is typically located in the account management or privacy section.

Step 3: Confirm Your Decision

Tap on the Delete Account button. A confirmation dialog will appear asking you to confirm this action. Read the warning carefully before proceeding.

Step 4: Final Confirmation

Confirm your decision to proceed. Once confirmed, your account and all associated data are permanently deleted from our servers through a secure API process.

Step 5: Automatic Redirect

After deletion is complete, you will be automatically redirected to the Login screen. Your account has now been successfully removed.

üîí What Happens After Deletion

Once you delete your account, the following actions take place immediately:

  • Complete Data Removal: All user data associated with your account is permanently removed from our servers and cannot be recovered.
  • No Personal Information Retained: No personal information is retained on our servers after the deletion process is complete.
  • Secure Deletion Process: Your data is deleted through a secure API process that ensures complete removal from all our systems.
  • Immediate Effect: The deletion takes effect immediately, and you will no longer have access to your account.
  • Future Use: If you wish to use the app again in the future, you will need to create a new account from scratch.

üîê Data Protection & Privacy

We take your privacy seriously. When you delete your account:

  • All personal data is removed from our production databases
  • Backup systems are purged according to our retention policies
  • Third-party integrations are notified to remove your data
  • Analytics and logs containing your information are anonymized
  • No trace of your account remains in our active systems

‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

Can I recover my account after deletion?

No, account deletion is permanent and irreversible. Once deleted, your account and all associated data cannot be recovered.

How long does the deletion process take?

The deletion process is immediate. Your account and data are removed from our servers as soon as you confirm the deletion.

Will I receive a confirmation email?

If you have an email associated with your account, you may receive a confirmation email notifying you that your account has been deleted.

Can I create a new account with the same email?

Yes, after your account is deleted, you can create a new account using the same email address. However, none of your previous data will be available.

What if I change my mind?

Once you confirm the deletion, the process cannot be stopped or reversed. Please make sure you want to delete your account before confirming.
